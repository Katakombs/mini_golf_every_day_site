Meet the Creator

Hi, I’m MJ — the creator of Mini Golf Every Day. I grew up in New Jersey, and some of my favorite memories are from Ocean City, where my family spent countless summer days. It was on that boardwalk, at Tee Time Golf, that I first fell in love with mini golf. The course is still there — nearly unchanged — and it holds a special place in my heart.

The idea for Mini Golf Every Day actually started with a funny misunderstanding. I told a friend that we played mini golf every day while on vacation, and they thought I meant we played every single day of the year. That little mix-up sparked something, and on January 1st, 2025, I decided to go for it; to really play mini golf every single day.

Now, with my husband, four amazing kids and two super cute dogs often joining me, this project has become a celebration of family, fun, and consistency. Through each video, I get to share not just my love of mini golf, but also pieces of my personality, joy, and the things that matter most to me. Whether it's a themed putt, a goofy challenge, or a simple game at a local course, Mini Golf Every Day is about more than just golf; it's about making each day a little brighter.
